Movement Control Order (MCO)

This rule implemented as to prevent from corona virus pandemic start on 18 March 2020. When the government announced
the Movement Control Order, as a Malaysian we need to obey on that rule as to cut the virus from spreading widely.

A lot of thing happens during the Movement Control Order as we cannot going out from house. I spend doing great
activites and that give me new experince to explore during the period of Movement Control Order such as:





Making the Viral Coffee, Dalgona Coffee

This is my version of dalgona coffee. Dalgona Coffee went viral on social media as the coffee looks nice with the frothy cream on top and the ingredients are surely
available in every house. This coffee known through one of variety show in Korean and everyone wants to try this as the way we have to whisk the coffee
which is take time to make it become thick. As people stay at home and have time to kill by doing this coffee.

Ingredients of Dalgona Coffee

  • 2 Tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 2 Tablespoon of sugar
  • 2 Tablespoon of hot water
  • 2-3 Ice cubes
